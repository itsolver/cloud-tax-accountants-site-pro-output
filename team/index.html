Meet The Team

Zeenet Scarborough

Director

Zeenet Scarborough is a Certified Practicing Accountant (CPA) with more than 15 years experience in tax, accounting, consulting and business advice.

Let’s work together to start achieving them.

Bec Buttery

Administration Assistant

Bec has always had a passion for helping people and was previously a nurse at the Mater Private Hospital.  

Bec is dedicated to providing high quality, personalised customer service and problem solving.

Outside of the office, Bec is a committed member of the Redlands community who you will see volunteering and dedicating time to assist others.  Bec’s interest include raising her three daughters to be strong independent women, mindfulness of self and others and learning the ukulele.

Belinda Hornal

Senior Accountant

Belinda is a Chartered Accountant with 20 years of experience in tax consulting and business advice. 

Belinda specialises in general taxation advice, self-managed superannuation funds, QBCC and assisting small to medium businesses with all their accounting needs.

She enjoys helping clients navigate all aspects of their taxation and other business requirements in a straightforward, uncomplicated manner.

Belinda has lived and worked in the Redlands her whole life and loves the lifestyle the Redlands brings.
