Meet The Team

Zeenet Scarborough (She/Her)

Director

Zeenet is our Director and the Founder of Cloud Tax Accountants & Business Advisors.

Zeenet is a Certified Practicing Accountant (CPA) with more than 20 years experience in tax, accounting, consulting and business advice.

Her qualifications include:

  • Certified Practicing Accountant (CPA);
  • Registered Tax Agent;
  • Bachelor of Commerce (Accounting Major);
  • Public Practice Certificate;
  • Public Practice License;
  • Certified Mental Heath First Aider.

Zeenet is the proud mum of an amazing son who you will occasionally see in the office during the school holidays.

Outside of the office, Zeenet loves to read, swim and spend time at the beach on her paddle board.

Zeenet’s passion is to help individuals and businesses reach their goals.

“We are here to help you achieve your goals. Whatever they may be. Let’s work together to start achieving them”.
