Fringe Benefits Tax (FBT)

Each year on 31 March, the Fringe Benefit Tax (FBT) year comes to an end.  Fringe benefits are non-monetary benefits provided by a business to its employees, owners and associates.  The most common benefits are cars, entertainment, and the payment of expenses such as internet and mobile phone bills. To ensure FBT obligations are met, we send all of our Business Clients an FBT Questionnaire on 1 April each year.
