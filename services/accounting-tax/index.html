Accounting & Taxation

Here at Cloud Tax Accountants & Business Advisors, all of your Accounting and Taxation needs will be attended to by a fully qualified and highly experienced accountant based right here in our Victoria Point office.

From Individual Income Tax Returns, to the most complex Business Financial Statements, we have you covered.

Tax Return Preparation

We professionally prepare Tax Returns for:

  • Individuals (Wage & Salary Earners);
  • Sole Traders (Small Businesses & Contractors);
  • Partnerships;
  • Companies;
  • Trusts (Unit Trusts & Discretionary Trusts);
  • Self-Managed Super Funds.

We have specialist knowledge in relation to complex taxation matters including:

  • Crypto Currency
  • Capital Gains 
  • Investments (e.g. Rental Properties, Managed Funds, Shares, etc)
  • Influencers, Games & YouTubers.

Financial Statements Preparation

We prepare Financial Statements that are professionally prepared by our qualified team that compliant with either Special Purpose Financial Reporting or General Purpose Financial Reporting obligations.  

These are prepared for:

  • Individuals (Wage & Salary Earners);
  • Sole Traders (Small Businesses & Contractors);
  • Partnerships;
  • Companies;
  • Trusts (Unit Trusts & Discretionary Trusts);
  • Self-Managed Super Funds.
