QBCC Reporting & Applications

We have a unique understanding of QBCC and it’s reporting obligations as well as its application process.

With the changes that QBCC implemented on 1 July 2022, there is now a very specific way that financial information must be supplied to them. 

To accommodate these changes, we have undertaken the necessary training and implemented the required software so that General Purpose Financial Reports can be supplied to QBCC when required.

If your business operates within a category that requires a QBCC license, we are well placed to guide you through this minefield.

Cloud Tax Acccountant can assist with the QBCC licensing application process by performing the following tasks:

  1. Reviewing financials:  We can review your financials and make sure they meet the QBCC’s requirements for licensing.
  2. Preparation of financial statements: We can prepare financial statements, such as Balance Sheet, Profit & Loss Statements and Cash Flow Statements, to demonstrate your financial stability and capacity to undertake building work.
  3. Assisting with paperwork: We can help you complete the required paperwork accurately and on time. We can also assist with the preparation of supporting documentation, such as Tax Returns.
  4. Advising on financial requirements: We can advise you on the financial requirements for obtaining a QBCC license.
  5. Liaising with the QBCC: We can also act as a liaison between you and the QBCC, answering any questions and ensuring that the application process runs smoothly.

We provide valuable assistance in ensuring that your QBCC license application is complete, accurate and meets all the necessary financial requirements. This can help increase your chances of a successful outcome.
