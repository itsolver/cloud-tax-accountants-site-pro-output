Business Structuring

As there are a number of different ways to Structure your unique business, we will provide you with up-to-date information so that you understand the difference between all of the options and help you make the right decision from the start.

The structuring options could include:

  • Sole Trader;
  • Company;
  • Unit Trust;
  • Discretionary Trust;
  • Partnership;
  • Self-Managed Super Fund.

We are here to help you make the right decision for your unique circumstances.

We attend to all Structure establishments and set ups along with any associated registrations including:

  • Tax File Number Application;
  • ACN Application;
  • ABN Application;
  • QBCC Application;
  • GST Application;
  • PAYG Withholding Application;
  • Fuel Tax Credit Application;
  • Fringe Benefits Tax Application.
